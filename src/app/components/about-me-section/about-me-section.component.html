About me

My Story

Hi, I'm Shane, a 24 year old software engineer from Dublin, Ireland.
I Studied B.Sc in Computer Applications and Software engineering (DC121) in DCU (Dublin City University) and currently work at Brightflag.

I've always had an interest in technology. I was always fascinated with how technology worked, how games or web applications were developed. As a result of this interest, I started programming in small doses during school and began to involve myself in the software community in general. School never gave me the knowledge from any direct course or module to understand how technology in general worked so I decided to pursue this interest and enroll in a Computer Science degree. Four years later, I've graduated with a first-class honors degree, and that fire to build more software projects continues to rage on.

My Hobbies and Interests

Like most children growing up during the 1990s and early 2000s, video games were a big part of my life. Upon playing my very first game, I felt an immediate attachment and love for it. Gaming was certainly the primary reason why I had a curiosity about Computer Science.

Similarly, music has also been a passion of mine. I play guitar on a regular basis. Often playing pieces from a variety of genres that I enjoy. Being creative with programming and working on side-projects is also certainly a passion. I also love contributing to Open Source in my spare time.

My Aspirations and Career Goals

I want to grow in all aspects of the software engineering field, whether that be improving my own engineering ability or simply improving my soft skills. I want to learn to understand all of the different facets under the Computer Science umbrella. Because of this particular interest, I already have built projects spanning many domains in the field, whether that be Web Application Development, Mobile development, Machine Learning modeling/Data Science and Game Development.

I want to be the best I can at my job and meet great people along the way. I also want to work for a company that which I care about the work they do and strive to pursue each and every day. Alternatively, setting up my own business is another ambition I hope to realise one day, but until that day, Let's see where the wind takes me.
